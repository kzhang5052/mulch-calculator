How the math works

Volume = area × depth. We convert your depth from inches to feet, multiply by the area, then divide cubic feet by 27 to get cubic yards (the unit bulk suppliers sell in). Bag count assumes the standard bag size for each material.

How many cubic yards of mulch do I need?

Multiply area (sq ft) by depth (ft), divide by 27. A 200 sq ft bed at 3 inches deep needs about 1.85 cubic yards.

How many bags in a cubic yard?

A cubic yard is 27 cubic feet. With 2-cu-ft mulch bags that's about 13.5 bags. Bulk delivery is usually cheaper above ~3 yards.

How much does it weigh?

Mulch ≈ 500 lb/yd³, compost ≈ 1,100, topsoil ≈ 2,200, sand/gravel ≈ 2,600–2,700. Check your vehicle's payload before hauling.
