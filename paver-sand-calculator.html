How much sand for pavers?

Bedding sand is normally 1 inch deep. Volume = length × width × depth (in feet) ÷ 27 for cubic yards. A 10×10 patio at 1 inch needs about 8.3 cubic feet (~0.3 yards). Buy a little extra for screeding and joint filling.

How thick should bedding sand be?

One inch. Thicker layers let pavers sink and shift, causing an uneven surface over time.

Leveling sand vs polymeric sand?

Leveling (bedding) sand goes under the pavers. Polymeric sand goes in the joints between them and hardens to resist weeds and washout.

Do I need gravel under the sand?

Yes — a compacted gravel base (see the Paver Base calculator) goes under the sand for proper drainage and support.
